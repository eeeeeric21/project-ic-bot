Privacy Policy - Aescul Helper

Last updated: March 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Aescul Helper ("we", "our", or "the Service") is an AI-powered health check-in assistant designed to support elderly individuals living independently. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Aescul Helper, we may collect:

  • Check-in responses: Your answers to daily health questions (via voice or text)
  • Health signals: Mentions of pain, distress, or other health concerns
  • Usage data: When you interact with the service (timestamps, session duration)
  • Contact information: If you register, your name and preferred contact method

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Conduct daily health check-ins and track your wellbeing
  • Detect potential health concerns (pain, distress, cognitive issues)
  • Send alerts to your designated case worker or caregiver if concerns are detected
  • Generate weekly health summary reports
  • Improve our AI responses and service quality

4. Information Sharing

Your information may be shared with:

  • Case workers/caregivers: Only those you designate will receive alerts and reports
  • Healthcare providers: If authorized by you or your designated contact

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure database storage (Supabase)
  • Access controls limiting who can view your data

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Data Retention

We retain your check-in data for as long as you use the service, and for a reasonable period thereafter for record-keeping purposes. You may request deletion at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

This service is designed for elderly individuals and is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the service. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.
