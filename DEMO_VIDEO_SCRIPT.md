## Production Notes

### Equipment Needed

**Essential:**
- Computer with screen recording
- Smartphone (for Telegram demo)
- Microphone (for narration)

**Software (All FREE):**
- OBS Studio - Screen recording
- DaVinci Resolve - Video editing
- Audacity - Audio recording

**Optional:**
- Stock footage/images ($20-50)
- Professional microphone ($50-100)

### Key Points to Emphasize

**✅ DO:**
- Use real Telegram bot (not fake)
- Show actual risk scores updating
- Type at natural speed
- Include Singlish phrases ("lah", "hor", "ah")
- Be honest about PWA (chat only)
- Emphasize mobile app testing for Alexa

**❌ DON'T:**
- Fake any functionality
- Speed through conversations
- Claim PWA has dashboard
- Overpromise on timeline
- Hide implementation status
- Suggest Alexa needs Echo device

### Filming Environment

**For Screen Recording:**
- Clean desktop background
- Close unnecessary apps
- Use phone simulator or real device
- Good lighting (if showing hands)

**For Narration:**
- Quiet room
- Good microphone
- Natural, warm tone
- Speak clearly

## Tips for Success

### Narration Tips
- Speak slowly and clearly
- Use warm, empathetic tone
- Pause between key points
- Emphasize important words
- Sound confident but genuine

### Visual Tips
- Keep text on screen long enough to read
- Use high contrast colors
- Avoid cluttered backgrounds
- Use smooth transitions
- Show actual working features

### Demo Tips
- Test everything before recording
- Have backup plan if bot is slow
- Show realistic typing speed
- Include mistakes (more authentic)
- Keep energy high throughout
